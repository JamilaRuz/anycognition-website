Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects do you take on?

We work on AI automation, custom software development, mobile apps, web platforms, and startup MVP launches. If you have a unique project, reach out - we love tackling new challenges.

What's your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on scope, but most projects range from 4-12 weeks. During our discovery call, we'll provide a more accurate estimate for your specific needs.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We specialize in helping startups go from idea to market-ready MVP. We understand the unique challenges of early-stage companies and offer flexible engagement models.

Can you work with our existing development team?

Yes! We can integrate seamlessly with your existing team, whether you need us to lead development, provide specialized expertise, or fill specific skill gaps.

What's your pricing model?

We offer both fixed-price projects and flexible hourly arrangements, depending on your needs. We'll discuss the best approach during our discovery call and provide transparent pricing in our proposal.
